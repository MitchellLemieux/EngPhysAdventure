How to Convert python 2to3
Where python2ugly.py is the example script

Just Changes
C:python37/python.exe C:/python37/Tools/scripts/2to3.py python2ugly.py

Overwrite
C:python37/python.exe C:/python37/Tools/scripts/2to3.py -w python2ugly.py

Overwrite Changes in the CWD
C:python37/python.exe C:/python37/Tools/scripts/2to3.py -w .
